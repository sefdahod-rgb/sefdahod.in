About Us

Shubhi Education Foundation is committed to supporting education and social welfare in Dahod. Shubhi Education Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, social welfare, and sustainable development initiatives. We work with a vision to create equal opportunities for every individual, especially children from economically weaker sections. Through our programs, we aim to provide access to quality education, financial support, and awareness for a better future. Our foundation believes in transparency, dedication, and long-term impact, ensuring that every effort brings meaningful change to society.

Our Mission: Our mission is to empower individuals and communities by providing access to education, support, and opportunities for growth. We are committed to helping underprivileged children continue their education, supporting families in need, and creating awareness about health, hygiene, and social issues. We aim to build a society where every person has the chance to learn, grow, and live with dignity.

Our Vision: Our vision is to create a society where no child is deprived of education and every individual has equal opportunities to succeed. We aspire to build a future that is educated, self-reliant, and socially responsible.

Our Goal: 1. Provide education support to needy students. 2. Organize health camps and awareness programs 3. Promote skill development and self-employment 4. Support poor families with essential resources 5. Encourage youth participation in social work

Our Impact: 500+ Students Supported . 1000+ Families Helped . 50+ Awareness Programs Conducted . Continuous CSR and Donation Support
